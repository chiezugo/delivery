Ocean Freight Service

Ocean freight is the main facilitator for international shipping it allows to make shipments of large amount of cargo. Ocean freight can be done in many ways, like container, bulk cargo etc. The most common shipping in international freight is ocean freight by container.

There are two ways in ocean shipping:

  • FCL (Full Container Load)
  • LCL (Less than a Container Load)

FCL (Full Container Load)

We offer wide range of containers; every container is designed to meet different needs. These can be fully enclosed, temperature-controlled or simply a flat base for oversize goods. We use Full Container Loads (FCL) to offer our customers reliable, flexible and cost efficient transportation.

LCL (Less than a Container Load)

We offer Less Than Container Load (LCL) cargo to every major container port around the world. Departure schedules are well managed from end-to-end, to ensure customer's requirements and promises are kept. In addition to our standard LCL shipping service, customer can take advantage of our completive priced door pickup, cargo insurance to tailor every LCL shipment to customer's specific needs.
