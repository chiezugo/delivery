Our Services

Our services include armored car transportation, money processing, long-distance transport of valuables, vaulting and other value-added solutions. DML Courier Servce specializes in the secure transportation and handling of valuable goods throughout the logistics value chain, from raw materials and components to finished products within the mining, bank note, precious metals, jewelry, security, art and pharmaceutical industries. DML Courier Servce provides such value added services as Customs Clearance and Brokerage, Inventory Management, Trade Show Services, Cash Processing, Walk-in Service Centers, Free Trade Zones and Bonded Warehouses.
